# Twala — Product Requirements Document (PRD)
"Carrying Home Across Borders"

## 1. Product Overview
Twala is an intelligent cross-border financial companion that helps Ugandans living abroad send, manage, and grow their money back home. It focuses on *why* users send money (goals) rather than just the transaction.

## 2. Core Problem
High remittance costs, lack of visibility/tracking, and difficulty managing long-term projects (like building a house) from abroad.

## 3. Core Features
- **AI Financial Companion**: Understands and plans for goals (Construction, Savings, Family).
- **Goal-Based Management**: Tracks progress toward specific life goals.
- **Smart Transfers**: Stellar-powered (USDC to UGX) for speed and low cost.
- **Trusted Payments**: Controlled releases for project milestones.
- **AI Fraud Protection**: Detects unusual activity.
- **Transparent History**: Immutable records with purpose-tracking.

## 4. MVP Scope
- AI companion interaction.
- Goal creation and dashboard.
- Stellar testnet integration (simulated).
- Goal progress tracking.
- Transaction history.
